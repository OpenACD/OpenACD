get_best_bindable_queues/0

get_best_bindable_queues() -> [{string(), pid(), {any(), #queued_call{}} | none, pos_integer()}]

Sort queues containing a bindable call. The queues are sorted from most important to least by weight, priority of first bindable call, then by the time the first bindable call has been in queue, influenced by queue service time.

get_queue/1

get_queue(Name::string()) -> pid() | undefined
